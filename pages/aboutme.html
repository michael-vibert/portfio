About Me.

Hello, I’m Michael and I enjoy making seamless, friction free and aesthetic places for people to chill out or get things done. My interest in coding started back when I was building a WordPress site for my business in 2017. I didn’t realise it at the time but exploring the back end of the C-Panel was building my understanding of HTML and CSS.

Currently I’m studying Full Stack Web Development at Coder Academy. Just prior to that I was studying Cyber Security at UNSW, Computer Science at Monash and CyberSec/Networking at MIT.

In my previous life I was a practicing Chiropractor registered with APRHA. After 8 years of serving the community and running my own small business I made the big decision and follow my passion in Tech.

My current fascinations include:

  • Javascript
  • React
  • JQuery
  • Python
  • Flask

Outside of work and study I am an active member of the local cycling community. I enjoy getting involved with events and races and have a fantastic and supportive partner with whom I spend time with exploring in nature. And I also enjoy getting my hands dirty in home DIY renovation madness!
